About Us

International Affilliates

Proguard Security Services, is a limited liability company that is registered under the Corporate Laws of the Republic of Ghana. Proguard Security Services is a Shipping and Logistics organization with a wide range of expertise in Shipping services, Cargo handling, Transportation, Customs Brokerage, Import and Export, Transit, Warehousing and Ex – Warehousing and Consultancy services. Proguard Security Services was established in 1997 with operational licenses from Ghana Shippers Council, Ghana Port and Harbours Authority, Ghana Institute of Freight Forwarders (GIFF) and Ghana Customs Excise and Preventive Service (CEPS).

In Ghana, Proguard Security Serviceshas offices in the Ports of Tema and Takoradi, Kotoka International Airport (KIA) and at Aflao..

Proguard Security Services is a member of JCTRANS (Global Alliance of International Freight Forwarders and Logistics Service providers)

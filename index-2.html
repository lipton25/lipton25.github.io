Welcome to Proguard Security Service

Proguard Security Service, is a limited liability company that is registered under the Corporate Laws of the Republic of Ghana.

Proguard Security Service is a Shipping and Logistics organization with a wide range of expertise in Shipping Service, Cargo handling, Transportation, Customs Brokerage, Import and Export, Transit, Warehousing and Ex â€“ Warehousing and Consultancy Service.

Proguard Security Service was established in 1997 with operational licenses from Ghana Shippers Council, Ghana Port and Harbors Authority, Ghana Institute of Freight Forwarders (GIFF) and Ghana Customs Excise and Preventive Service (CEPS).

In Ghana, Proguard Security Service has offices in the Ports of Tema and Takoradi, Kotoka International Airport (KIA) and at Aflao.
